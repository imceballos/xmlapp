Welcome to the Help Section!

In this section, you will find all the information you need to use our platform easily and effectively. If you have any questions or issues, feel free to consult them here! Regarding the login process, it's very simple. You just need to enter your username and password on the login screen and click "Sign in." If you don't remember your login information, you can use the "Forgot password" option to reset it.Once you have logged in, you can see all the available tasks, including pending requests for files that need to be accepted or rejected.Remember that on our platform, you can send and receive files securely and reliably.

If you have any other questions or issues, don't hesitate to contact us through our contact form. We'll be happy to assist you with whatever you need.
